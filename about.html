About Us

We are proud leaders in the Nephrology medical community in Central Ohio. We are dedicated to improving the quality of life, your life, through dedicated, hands-on, leading-edge medical technology and compassion. Individually and as a team, we have pledged in treating our patients and our own coworkers with respect, honesty and professionalism. We are Columbus Nephrology and we are proud to serve our community.

Our team of board-certified physicians treats patients with the following hypertension and kidney conditions or needs:

  • Acute kidney failure
  • Chronic kidney disease
  • Blood chemistry disorders
  • Anemia
  • Kidney replacement therapy
  • Post kidney transplant care
  • Dialysis
  • Dialysis access care and interventional procedures (Vascular Access Center of Central Ohio)
